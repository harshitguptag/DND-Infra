About Us

DND Infra (formerly M/S S.D Constructions) is a respected construction company known for delivering high-quality projects within budget and on time. They have received accolades and repeat business due to their commitment to excellence.
DND Infra focuses on creating important infrastructure assets, providing innovative and cost-effective solutions, expanding their portfolio, and maintaining safety, health, environment, and quality management systems.
They strive for continuous improvement, ensure quality performance through an in-house site lab, and aim to build lasting relationships with clients.
